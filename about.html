About US

RenTech Roots

RenTech started out in early spring, 2014.

RenTech is where the world goes to rent equipment. Our mission is to be the world’s favorite destination for discovering great value and unique selection. Its the platform that lets you rent out your belongings to others nearby. Everyday, we connect people who need things, with others in their neighborhood that have it to lend.


We believe in getting people to work together to make the world a more efficient place for everyone. So we’re making it quick and easy for people to share their belongings. Our dream is a platform where all goods - from the everyday to the abstract - can be borrowed within minutes.
